Why ThrowIT?

At present in India, there is no automated waste segregated approach at the household/corporate level and a compact, low cost and user friendly segregation system for urban settings is the need of the hour.

ThrowIT's fully automatic waste segregation uses a Deep Learning Algorithm. It involves acquiring images, prediction and classification.

  • Better segregation at source
  • Minimizing human intervention
  • Effective Recycling
  • Environment Savvy

Facts at a Glance

Waste Generated

A staggering figure of 43 million tonnes of Solid Waste is collected annually, out of which only 11.9 million, that is 22-28% is treated, while demo 31 million tonnes of waste is left untreated and dumped at the landfill sites.

Metro Trash Management

The capital city of Delhi generates around 9,000 metric tonnes of waste every day and is already sitting on a ticking garbage bomb! Delhi has a total of 3 landfills, at Ghazipur, Okhla and Bhalswa.

Land of Landfills

India’s landfills are bursting at the seams and overflowing with items that shouldn’t be thrown in the trash. More than 70% of collected urban waste is dumped straight into the landfills.

Untreated Waste

Imagine 3 million trucks piled high with garbage. That’s how much untreated garbage Urban India generates every day. That's 1/2 the distance between moon and earth if these trucks are laid end to end.

Pollution Bombs

Apart from overflowing waste, India’s landfills are also home to toxins, leachate and greenhouse gases. With time, toxins produced by waste leaches into the soil and groundwater, and become environmental hazards for years.

Lack of Awareness and Enforcement

The solid waste management, 2016 has made waste segregation mandatory for every waste generator be it an individual, community, society or an corporate office, but implementation remains poor.
